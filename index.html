1. Overview

AxionOX ("we," "our," or "us") is designed with privacy as its core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled within our fully offline medication management application. Crucially, AxionOX does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any of your personal data to our servers or any third parties.

This Policy applies to all users of the AxionOX mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to the local data handling practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

AxionOX operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers. All data resides solely on your device.

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide medication tracking functionality, the App stores the following information directly on your device using local database storage:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Medication Data Drug names, dosages, frequencies, schedules Core medication reminder functionality
Dose Logs Records of taken/missed doses, timestamps Tracking adherence history
App Settings Reminder preferences, display options Personalizing your experience

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No Account Required: There is no registration process, so we never collect your name, email, or phone number.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the App or your navigation patterns.
  • No Location Data: We do not request or access your location information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since all data remains on your device, "use" refers strictly to local processing:

  • Local Functionality: To generate reminders, calculate schedules, and display your medication history.
  • Device Notifications: To trigger local push notifications for your scheduled doses.
  • Data Persistence: To save your entries so they are available the next time you open the App.

Note: None of this information ever leaves your device.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data because we do not have access to it. Since AxionOX operates offline and does not utilize cloud servers, there is no data transmitted to us or any third-party analytics/services.

In the rare event of a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition), we would update this policy accordingly. However, your local data remains unaffected unless you choose to transfer it.

5. Local Storage & Tracking Technologies

AxionOX uses standard local storage mechanisms provided by your mobile operating system (such as SQLite databases or UserDefaults) to save your data on your device.

  • No Cookies: We do not use web cookies.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: We do not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that track you across other apps or websites.
  • No Network Calls: The App does not initiate any network connections.

6. Data Security

Your data security relies primarily on the security features of your own device (passcodes, biometrics, encryption). We enhance privacy through:

  • Offline Architecture: By design, your data is never exposed to internet-based threats or server breaches.
  • No Cloud Sync: Eliminates risks associated with cloud storage vulnerabilities.
  • Minimal Permissions: We only request permissions essential for local notifications (if required by OS).

We recommend securing your device with a strong passcode and keeping your OS updated to protect your local data.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Access & Correction: Access and modify your data directly within the App.
  • Deletion: Delete individual records within the App, or uninstall the App to remove all associated data from your device.
  • Portability: If export features are available, you may export your data in standard formats.

For any questions regarding your data handling, contact us at axionoxm5wi@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only on your device until you decide to delete it.

  • Active Use: Data persists locally while the App is installed.
  • Uninstallation: Deleting the App typically removes all locally stored data, subject to your device's OS behavior.
  • Backups: If you use device backup services (e.g., iCloud, Google Drive), data may be included in those backups according to your backup settings.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under 13, specifically because it does not collect personal information online. If you are a parent or guardian, you control the device and the data stored on it. We do not knowingly collect any data from children online.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the App or on our website. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
